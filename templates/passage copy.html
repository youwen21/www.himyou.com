Part 1 Reading Comprehension
阅读理解 Passage 1
They won't get you drunk, but some experts say low-alcohol and alcohol-free beer drinks shouldn't be sold to minors, and they're calling for laws to prevent sales to kids and teens. 译 词
它们不会让你醉，但一些专家说低酒精和无酒精啤酒饮料不应该卖给未成年人，他们呼吁立法禁止向儿童和青少年销售。
drunk
/drʌŋk/
adj. 喝醉的；醉酒的；沉醉的；着迷的
n. 酗酒者，酒鬼
v. 喝，饮；喝酒（drink 的过去分词形式）
Surveys of primary, middle, and high school students in Japan, where the legal drinking age is 20, found that 20% to 30% said they were drinking non-alcoholic beverages(饮品). Additional studies in Japan found that primary school students who said they drank non-alcoholic drinks were more interested in drinking alcohol than those who said they didn't have these kinds of drinks. 译 词
在日本（法定饮酒年龄为20岁），对小学、初中和高中学生的调查发现，20%到30%的学生表示他们在饮用无酒精饮料。日本的进一步研究发现，表示饮用无酒精饮料的小学生比表示没有饮用这类饮料的学生更有兴趣饮酒。
survey
/ˈsɜːrveɪ/
n. 调查，研究
In Australia, where the legal drinking age is 18, researchers conducted focus groups and surveyed teens aged 15 to 17 about the use of what they called zero-alcohol drinks. (76) They found that more than a third had tried zero-alcohol drinks, and that more than 1 in 5 were drinking them at least monthly. In the survey, teens who said they had tried zero-alcohol drinks were 2.5 times more likely to also have drunk alcohol compared with those who'd never had them. 译 词
在澳大利亚（法定饮酒年龄为18岁），研究人员对15至17岁的青少年进行了焦点小组讨论和调查，了解他们使用所谓的零酒精饮料的情况。研究发现超过三分之一的人尝试过零酒精饮料，超过五分之一的人至少每月饮用一次。在调查中，表示尝试过零酒精饮料的青少年比从未尝试过的青少年更有可能饮酒，概率高出2.5倍。
conduct
/kənˈdʌkt/
v. 进行，实施
Dr. Leon Booth said teens appear to be drinking these for various reasons. Some were just curious about new products and tried them but didn't drink them frequently. Others, though, said they had used zero-alcohol drinks to fit in with older friends who were drinking. "They are effectively role-playing drinking when they choose a zero-alcohol version(类型) of an alcoholic product, instead of a regular soft drink or something else obviously not alcoholic." Booth wrote in an email. 译 词
Leon Booth博士说青少年饮用这些饮料的原因各不相同。有些人只是对新产品感到好奇，尝试过但不经常饮用。但其他人表示，他们使用零酒精饮料是为了融入正在饮酒的年长朋友群体。Booth在邮件中写道："当他们选择某种酒精饮品的零酒精版本，而不是普通软饮料或其他明显不含酒精的饮料时，他们实际上是在模仿饮酒行为。"
various
/ˈveəriəs/
adj. 各种各样的，不同的
"In the focus group discussions, a few teens mentioned they had gotten used to the taste of beer by drinking zero-alcohol versions, which suggests that zero-alcohol beverages can help them to adjust to the taste of alcoholic beer," he added. 译 词
他补充说："在焦点小组讨论中，一些青少年提到他们通过饮用零酒精版本逐渐适应了啤酒的味道，这表明零酒精饮料可以帮助他们适应含酒精啤酒的味道。"
adjust
/əˈdʒʌst/
v. 适应，调整
The Distilled Spirits Council said, "Our members agree that non-alcoholic beverages that are made to look like the real thing shouldn't be consumed by kids and teens." 译 词
蒸馏酒协会说："我们的成员一致认为，那些制作得像真酒一样的无酒精饮料不应该被儿童和青少年饮用。"
consume
/kənˈsjuːm/
v. 消费，饮用
