Part I Reading Comprehension
Passage 1
For most of us, having drinking water is a privilege we often take for granted. Yet in several regions of Africa, there is serious shortage of freshwater. Researchers have been developing methods to collect moisture from fog for daily water use as if it were rainwater. The difference is that fog is simply more common than rain. Fog forms when the land releases heat at night. As the ground cools down, it causes water molecules in the air to condense.
对于我们大多数人来说，拥有饮用水是一种常被视为理所当然的特权。然而，在非洲的多个地区，淡水资源严重短缺。研究人员一直在开发从雾中收集水分的方法，以便将其作为日常用水，就像利用雨水一样。不同之处在于，雾比雨更为常见。雾是在夜间地表释放热量时形成的。随着地面冷却，会导致空气中水分子凝结。
Fog collection works by utilizing mesh(网状物) hung in the air. The mesh is spread between two poles facing the general direction of the wind. The wind naturally pushes the fog toward the mesh, where water accumulates. As the water drops get bigger, they flow down to a pipe into a tank. This method can produce around 20 gallons(加仑) of water per mesh, which is enough for a family's daily needs. The entire system is also low-maintenance without the need for electricity.
雾收集工作原理是通过悬挂在空中的网来实现的。网被展开固定在两根杆子之间，面向风大致吹来的方向。风自然地将雾推向网，使得水在那里积聚。随着水滴变大，它们会流下并通过管道进入储水箱。这种方法每个网大约可以产生20加仑的水，足以满足一个家庭的日用水需求。整个系统也不需要电力，维护成本很低。
The steady supply of water has allowed communities to develop in many other ways. Locals no longer fall victim to water anxiety or the constant worry of finding water. Women can spend more time taking care of their children instead of walking long distances to collect water. The improved facilities have also opened opportunities long distances to collect water. The improved facilities have also opened opportunities for men to work consistent jobs.
稳定水源的供应使得社区在许多其他方面得到了发展。当地人不再遭受缺水焦虑或持续寻找水源的担忧。妇女可以花费更多时间照顾孩子，而无需长途跋涉去取水。改善的设施也为男性提供了从事稳定工作的机会。
(76) While this solution has helped many communities globally, it presents future questions. For one, such systems work best in rural regions. They don't work in cities for lack of space. Secondly, atmospheric water only makes up 0.004% of the freshwater found on the planet. Therefore, this system can only help small communities. Furthermore, climate change also affects the amount of fog in a region's climate, so there may also be a limit to how long this system can work.
尽管这一解决方案已帮助全球众多社区，但它也引发了未来的一些问题。首先，这样的系统在乡村地区最为有效。由于缺乏空间，它们在城市中无法发挥作用。其次，大气水仅占地球上淡水的0.004%，因此该系统只能帮助较小的社区。此外，气候变化也会影响某一地区气候中的雾量，所以该系统能持续运作的时间也可能存在限制。
