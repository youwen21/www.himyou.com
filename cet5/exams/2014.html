Part I Oral Communication (15 minutes, 10points)
Section A

Dialogue One
A: I thought you said there were three men
B: They had been in there for about 5 minutes
C: It's the other man I am talking about
Burney: There were two men, I think. No, three. They ran into the bank and the one with the gun, the tall one, he runs up to the window, and starts shouting something, I don't know, "Give me all your money" and the other one...
Police officer: ____(1)?
Burney: No, there were two men and a girl. ____(2), the one carrying the suitcase, well, he goes up to the other guy.
Police officer: The one with the gun?
Burney: Yes, and he opens the suitcase and the cashier, well, she — well, all the other people behind the window — they hand over piles of money and two men put it into the suitcase and they run out. It was 1:35. ____(3).
